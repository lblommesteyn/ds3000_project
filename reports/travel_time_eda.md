## Key Takeaways

- Travel-time observations are densest around 17:00 with median segment times peaking near 188.0 seconds.
- Weekday congestion is materially higher than weekends (weekday mean=198.2s vs weekend mean=180.2s).
- Long-tail segments (top 15 by 90th percentile) maintain 90th percentile travel times above 494 seconds, flagging candidates for deeper slowdown investigation.
- Data coverage thins after late 2016; the most recent observed month is 2017-02.
